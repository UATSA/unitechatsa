President

KEY ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • The president provides spiritual leadership by guiding Bible studies, prayer meetings, and evangelistic programs.
  • They oversee administration and governance, ensuring smooth operations, delegating tasks, and maintaining church relations.
  • They promote evangelism and mission work, organizing outreach programs and encouraging student participation in ministry.
  • They support student welfare and discipleship, mentoring students, fostering unity, and advocating for their needs.

Vice-President

Vice President - plays an important part in assisting the president and steps in the absent of president and work together cooperatively.

Secretary

KEY ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • The secretary manages official records and documentation, keeping minutes of meetings and maintaining important files.
  • They handle correspondence and communication, ensuring smooth interaction between the association, church leaders, and members.
  • They assist in event planning and coordination, organizing schedules, venues, and logistics for programs and meetings.
  • They oversee membership records and reporting, keeping track of members, attendance, and activities.
  • They ensure administrative efficiency, supporting the president and executive team in running the association effectively.

Treasurer

KEY ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • The treasurer manages the association’s finances, including budgeting, fundraising, and financial planning.
  • They record and track all financial transactions, ensuring transparency and accountability.
  • They collect and manage funds, including offerings, donations, and membership fees.
  • They prepare financial reports, presenting updates to the executive committee and members.
  • They ensure proper use of funds, making sure all expenditures align with the association’s mission and goals.

Spiritual.Leader

KEY ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • The spiritual leader guides the spiritual growth of members through Bible studies, prayer meetings, and devotionals.
  • They organize evangelism and outreach programs, encouraging students to participate in missions and witnessing activities.
  • They provide spiritual counseling and mentorship, supporting students in their faith journey and personal challenges.
  • They plan and lead worship services, ensuring Sabbath programs, vespers, and fellowship meetings are spiritually enriching.
  • They promote unity and discipleship, fostering a Christ-centered community within the association.

Choir

KEY ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • The choir master trains and leads the choir, ensuring members develop their musical and vocal skills.
  • They select and organize songs for worship services, evangelistic programs, and special events.
  • They coordinate rehearsals and performances, ensuring the choir is wellprepared and disciplined.
  • They promote spiritual growth through music, encouraging members to sing with devotion and purpose.
  • They collaborate with church and event organizers, ensuring the choir effectively supports worship and outreach activities.

Fundraising

KEY ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • The fundraising leader plans and coordinates fundraising activities to support the association’s programs and projects.
  • They develop creative fundraising strategies, such as food sales, charity events, and sponsorship drives.
  • They engage with donors and sponsors, seeking financial support from church members, organizations, and well-wishers.
  • They manage and track funds raised, ensuring transparency and proper allocation of resources.
  • They motivate and involve members in fundraising efforts, fostering teamwork and commitment to the association’s mission.

Social Leader

KEY ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • The social leader plans and organizes social events, fostering fellowship and unity among members.
  • They coordinate recreational activities, such as sports, picnics, and community service projects.
  • They promote teamwork and inclusiveness, ensuring all members feel welcomed and engaged.
  • They facilitate networking and bonding, strengthening relationships within the association and with other groups.
  • They support spiritual and personal growth, balancing social interactions with the association’s mission and values.

Media Publicity Officer

KEY ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • The media & publicity officer manages the association’s public image, promoting activities through social media, posters, and announcements.
  • They create and distribute content, including videos, photos, and written updates for events and programs.
  • They handle communication and publicity, ensuring members and the public stay informed about upcoming events.
  • They document and archive association activities, preserving records through photography, videography, and reports.
  • They collaborate with church and student leaders, ensuring effective outreach and engagement through media platforms.

Transport Coordinator

KEY ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • The transport coordinator organizes and manages transportation for members during events, worship services, and outreach programs.
  • They arrange vehicle bookings and ensure availability of reliable transport for all scheduled activities.
  • They coordinate drivers and schedules, ensuring timely pickups and dropoffs for members.
  • They ensure safety and compliance, making sure vehicles are in good condition and adhere to transport regulations.
  • They assist in budgeting for transport costs, working with the treasurer to manage fuel, rentals, and maintenance expenses.
