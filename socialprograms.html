Fundraising

The Papua Adventist Students Association was officially established on the 17th of August, 2019. The objective was to unite and strengthen the faith of the Southern Region student who are studying in campus. PASA also focuses on developing students into leaders and responsible citizens of PNG who are God fearing and integral in their dealings. This year PASA will commemorate its 7th anniversary under the leadership of President Raga Vele and Vice-President Macknenna Wai.
