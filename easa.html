President

As President, I, Jeremy Alex, am committed to leading MEASA with faith, wisdom, and integrity, ensuring that our association remains a beacon of light for all students. Inspired by our theme, "Daniels in Babylon," I strive to create an environment where members can grow spiritually, academically, and socially while standing firm in their faith. My role is to provide direction, encourage unity, and ensure that all our programs align with our mission to glorify God. I believe that through fervent prayer, teamwork, and dedication, we can overcome challenges and positively impact every member of our association.

I work to ensure that every student feels included and empowered to take part in our activities, helping them build resilience like Daniel in Babylon.
