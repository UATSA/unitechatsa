President

Hi and greetings in the name of Jesus, As President, I am delighted to introduce you to the Papua Adventist Student Association (PASA). Our mission is to create a nurturing and spiritually enriching environment for all members, where everyone feels welcome and supported. This year, 2025, I am honoured to lead this association alongside Mackenna Wai and work hand in hand with all executives of this vibrant family to create an environment that reflects our shared values and beliefs.
Our focus will be on fostering a nurturing and spiritually enriching atmosphere where everyone feels welcome and supported. Together, we will engage in activities and events that promote academic excellence, community outreach, and spiritual growth."
PASA includes individuals from Western, Gulf, Milne Bay, and Central provinces. However, this does not restrict anyone or any student who wishes to join the team. Our focus is to get as many individuals as possible to know Christ thoroughly and let God dwell in their hearts so that together we can be saved when Christ returns.


Warm regards,

Raga VELE

Choir Master

It is a blessing for me to serve as the 2025 PASA Choir Master. Singing creates a bond between the students and strengthens our relationship with God. Our pioneer Choir Master Jack Vagi had composed several songs which are based upon our life experiences and struggles, hence our composed songs are our prayers.
I have always told my friends that PASA is a ship, Jesus is the Captain, and all of us students are the crew members. We shall sail through the storms of family problems, the waves of personally struggles will beat on us, and tempt us to follow the easier and exciting path of the world that will lead us astray. Nevertheless, we must always have faith that PASA is a ship and Jesus is the Captain. Thus, having Jesus as the captain and master of our life is a wonderful thing.
If you wish to sing choir with us, please send an email to unitech.atsa.info@gmail.com and express your interest. We can't wait to have you join or support our choir to praise God.


Blessings,

Gerard IVALAOA

Choir Master

Social Leader

The Unitech PASA Social Leader role is about keeping the vibes alive and making campus life fun and engaging! From planning exciting events to creating opportunities for students to connect, this role brings energy and positivity to the association. The goal is to build a welcoming, enlightening atmosphere where friendships grow and everyone feels at home. It’s not just about organizing activities—it’s about making PASA a family where faith, fun, and learning come together!
